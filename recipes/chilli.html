Easy Chilly recipe

Yummy Chilly recipe

You only need 5 ingredients for this crockpot Italian chicken with Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese. Nothing could be easier than this for a weekday meal that's ready when you get home.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces of tomato sauce
  • 15 ounces of can chilli beans
  • 2 tablespoons of chilli powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker, and pour dressing mixture over chicken.
  3. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sauce
  5. Bring to a boil and stir in chili powder. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 15 minutes
  6. Season to taste with salt and pepper
